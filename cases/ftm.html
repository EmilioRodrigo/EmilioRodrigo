Our goal was to provide a corporative and professional image. We designed the logo and their landing page, a place to know the team and the foundation.

01. CONCEPT

About the foundation.

Teresa Moretó Foundation is a non-profit organization that was born in order to make the results of biomedical research reach our society. They are a team of doctors with plenty of experience in biomedical research that have joined together to make this project posible.

02. OBJECTIVES

Sharing knowledge.

Aproaching the society with advenced medical investigation is not an easy goal, they want to contribute to our health and work together with doctors and other analists in order to make it posible for hospitals to use that knowledge.

03. VALORS

Having a personal identity.

What makes the foundation unique is the way the work with doctors and universities to transfer knowledge from one to others and make an actual impact on our society. As it is a foundation that works for the society their founders don't get any kind of economical profit. Different investigators are contributing to coordinate projects related to biomedical medicine and how they can help our health.

04. CONCLUSION

The future of biomedicine.

Teresa Moreto Foundation is not only evolved on cancer related projects, they also help collaborating on an active way on mental disease investigations.
