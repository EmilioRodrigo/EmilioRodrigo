Hello, my name is Emilio Rodrigo. I'm a Graphic & Web Designer from Barcelona.

ABOUT ME

I started my trip on the world of design after finishing my Business Administration career.

What impressed me the most about graphic & web design is the way it helps to solve real problems and its unique place in the world of communication.

As a designer I am very passionate and I like to focus on every small detail and learn about how it has been done and how it could be improved.

CASE STUDIES

Persovuses

Persovuses is an association that works for the integration and reintegration of people who are vulnerable or in situations of social exclusion.

I worked for them creating their new branding, website and in different social campaigns. Each of the products was designed in order to share a problem and make the society more conscious about it.

Teresa Moreto Foundation

Teresa Moretó Foundation is a non-profit organization that was born in order to make the results of biomedical research reach our society.

My goal was to help the foundation to make their first steps, I helped designing their logo and their first website.

Josep Mas i Dalmau & Estopa

The Residence is a center dedicated to the care of mentally handicapped persons built in 1991.

With the collaboration of the music group Estopa we tried to help the center raise founds in order to build a sense development room for their users. We designed a social media campaign and a website for the donations.
